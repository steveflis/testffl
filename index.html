About Flis Forest & Land, LLC

Flis Forest & Land, LLC is rooted in practical forestry and built on a foundation of both field experience and science. We specialize in sustainable timber management and habitat improvement for private landowners seeking to enhance their forest’s health and productivity.

With years of hands-on consulting and a deep understanding of state and federal conservation programs, we help landowners access funding through grants and develop long-term forest management plans. Our mission is to ensure your woods are not only profitable, but ecologically resilient — now and for generations to come.

  • Forest Management Planning
  • Habitat Restoration & Improvement
  • State & Federal Grant Guidance
