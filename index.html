About Flis Forest & Land, LLC

Flis Forest & Land, LLC provides forestry consulting services to private landowners seeking to improve their land's productivity, ecological health, and long-term value. With expertise in habitat improvement, grant programs, and sustainable timber management, we help you get the most out of your woods — without compromising their future.

Why Hire a Forester?

  • Increase timber value with strategic harvesting
  • Access state and federal funding through expert grant guidance
  • Restore wildlife habitat and promote ecological diversity
  • Protect long-term forest health and sustainability
  • Navigate regulations and planning with professional confidence

Services

  • Timber Cruising & Volume Estimates
    Accurate inventory of tree species, DBH, and marketable volume to assess stand value.
  • Forest Management Planning
    Custom plans tailored to landowner goals, species composition, and site-specific objectives.
  • Habitat Improvement
    Strategic treatments to improve game habitat, pollinator plots, and biodiversity corridors.
  • State & Federal Grant Assistance
    Help preparing and submitting grant applications for forest health and conservation funding.
  • Stand Mapping & Inventory
    GIS and boots-on-the-ground mapping to define stand boundaries and management units.
  • Landowner Education
    Walk-throughs, workshops, and direct consulting to empower informed land management decisions.
